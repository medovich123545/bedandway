Frequently asked questions

FAQ Sea View Hotels Tangier

Which hotel offers the best sea view in Tangier?

For a direct sea view with beach access, the Idou Malabata Beach & Spa (4.6/5) is the best choice. For a panoramic view of both the city AND the sea, the Fairmont Tazi Palace (4.7/5) is unmatched. Le Balcon de Tanger offers a unique view over two seas — the Atlantic and Mediterranean simultaneously.

What is the best time of year to visit Tangier?

The best time is spring (April–June) and autumn (September–October) with ideal temperatures between 20 and 26°C. Summer is warm but lively with a beautiful sea. Winter is mild (14–18°C), less crowded and with more accessible rates.

Are there hotels with a private beach in Tangier?

Yes. The Idou Malabata Beach & Spa has direct beach access in the Malabata area. The Hilton Al Houara Resort & Spa (20 km south) offers a near-private beach on the Atlantic, considered one of the most peaceful in the region.

Is it better to stay in Malabata or the city centre for sea views?

The Malabata area (Idou Malabata) offers immediate sea views and direct beach access, ideal for a beach holiday. The city centre (Barceló, Fairmont) gives access to restaurants, souks and the energy of Tangier with views over the strait. Both options have their advantages depending on your priorities.

How do I get to Tangier from the airport?

Ibn Batuta Airport is 15 km from the city centre. By taxi, expect approximately 100–200 MAD (€10–20). Some hotels such as the Fairmont and Hilton offer private transfer services. Always agree the fare with the driver before departing.

FAQ Transporte en Tánger

¿Cómo ir del aeropuerto de Tánger al centro?

El aeropuerto Ibn Battuta está a 15 km. La mejor opción: traslado privado (~25€ fijo). Taxi regulado: 150–250 MAD. Bus línea 17: ~5 MAD (lento).

¿Cuánto cuesta un taxi en Tánger?

Petit Taxi: mínimo 7 MAD (día) / 10 MAD (noche). Trayecto típico: 15–40 MAD (1,5–4€). Exige siempre el taxímetro. InDrive permite negociar precio antes.

¿Cuánto tarda el TGV Al Boraq a Casablanca?

2 horas y 10 minutos. Billete segunda clase ~20€. Reserva en Omio para billete digital.

¿Es recomendable alquilar un coche?

Sí, para Chefchaouen, Asilah, Cap Spartel. Carnet europeo válido. Desde 35€/día en DiscoverCars.

¿Funciona Uber en Tánger?

No. La alternativa es InDrive: propones el precio, el conductor acepta o contraoferta. Disponible 24h, pago efectivo o tarjeta. Descárgala antes de llegar.

¿Hay vuelos internos desde Tánger?

Sí. Marrakech: 1h10 desde 45€. Agadir: 1h25 desde 38€. Busca en Trip.com con 3 semanas de antelación.
